WHAT IS MYOTHERAPY?

A Myotherapist is a degree qualified 'hands-on' therapist. We use a variety of skills and techniques to treat muscles, joints and nerves. A session with a 'Myo' consists of a thorough assessment, which directs the choice of techniques used for treatment. A session with a 'Myo' can assist injury recovery, sports recovery or relieve tired, sore and overworked structures.
